Popular channel

A ready made channel at number 0 that loops the recent, top rated, and most watched movies and shows on your server. It works with no setup, and everything here lets you rename it, change its icon, cap its rating, and tune how it loops.
Disable to hide it without affecting your own channels.

Identity

Logo

Any Material Symbols name, drawn in the centre of the generated logo. Browse names at fonts.google.com/icons.

Playback

Bakes subtitles into the picture for everyone. Forced only burns the forced track, or the full track when the audio is in another language, while Always burns them on everything.

Age ratings

Limit which ratings air, optionally by time of day. No blocks means anything airs. On overlap, the lowest min and max win. The top titles are picked from what the rating allows.
Items starting this close to a window change must fit both windows. Set it to your longest content. 0 turns it off.

Guide

Tags the whole channel in the guide. Kids is set per rating block, and the movie tag is applied automatically while a movie is playing.

Episodes

Consecutive episodes per series before moving on. 1 disables grouping.
Keeps two part episodes together across a block boundary.

Content Types

Tagged in the guide while a movie is playing.
Season 0 specials.

Loop

How the channel arranges its content. Chronological plays oldest to newest by release date.
